Welcome to 2D-CAD Help Menu!

2D-CAD is a web app that can help you make polygon models. Things that you can do in 2D-CAD:

1. Draw lines, squares, rectangles, and polygons.

  1. Select ‘draw’ in ‘select mode’.

b. Under ‘select mode’, you will find ‘select shape’. Choose any shape you want, but here, we will draw a square.

c. Then, select a color or you can just put in an RGB color code.

d. Now we can start drawing.

e. Sometimes we just want to clear the canvas right? To do that, press ‘clear’ on the right of the ‘load’ button.

f. Need to leave? Don’t worry, you can always save it, and load it anytime you want! Just press the ‘save’ to save and the ‘load button to load. Your drawings are saved in JSON files.

2. Move your drawings

In ‘select mode’, select the ‘Move’ mode. After that, click on any pivot points that you want to move.

3. Color your model.

You can always change the colors of your drawings. For example, the drawing below is drawn in blue.

  1. Let’s change it to red and yellow. first, in ‘select mode’, select ‘paint’.

b. Select the appropriate color in ‘select color’. Here we will pick red.

c. Now click on the area in the drawing that you want to make red.

d. By repeating the same things for yellow, here is our red and yellow drawing.
